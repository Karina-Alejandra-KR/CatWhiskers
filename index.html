At Cat Whiskers, we are dedicated to improving the lives of street cats and kittens, offering them a chance for a better future. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide long-term care for abandoned and injured kittens living on the streets. We believe every kitten deserves a loving home, and we work tirelessly to find the right homes for each of them through our adoption programs.
